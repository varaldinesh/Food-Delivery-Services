Help & Support

Here at Food Delivery Service, we strive to provide the best possible service to our customers. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

FAQ

  • What are your business hours?

    Our business hours are from 9am to 9pm, seven days a week.

  • How can I place an order?

    You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply select the items you want and proceed to checkout.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal and Apple Pay.

  • How long does delivery take?

    Delivery times vary depending on your location and the time of day. You can check the estimated delivery time at checkout.

  • What is your return policy?

    We do not accept returns, but if you are not satisfied with your order, please contact us and we will do our best to make it right.
